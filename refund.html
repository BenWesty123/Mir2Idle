Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This policy explains how refunds work for token purchases in LOM Idle at www.lom2idle.com. It forms part of our Terms of Service.

1. Digital goods delivered immediately

Tokens are digital content that is delivered to your account instantly on payment. By buying tokens you request immediate delivery and acknowledge that, once tokens have been added to your account, you lose the 14-day "cooling-off" right to cancel that would otherwise apply to digital content under the UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

2. When we will give a refund

  • Non-delivery: you were charged but the tokens were not added to your account.
  • Duplicate charge: you were charged more than once for the same purchase.
  • Technical fault: a proven fault on our side prevented you from receiving or using the tokens you paid for.

In these cases we will either credit the missing tokens or refund the payment to your original payment method.

3. When we generally cannot give a refund

  • Tokens have already been spent or partly spent.
  • You changed your mind after the tokens were delivered.
  • You lost access to your account because you lost your recovery code.
  • Your progress was lost due to clearing your browser data without a saved recovery code.

This does not affect your statutory rights where the content is faulty or not as described.

4. How to request a refund

Email support@lom2idle.com within 30 days of the purchase. Please include the approximate date and time of the payment and the email address used at Stripe checkout, so we can locate the transaction. We aim to respond within 5 working days.

5. Chargebacks

Please contact us first so we can resolve any issue quickly. Opening a card chargeback without contacting us may result in the associated tokens being removed from your account.
